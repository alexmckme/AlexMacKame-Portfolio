About Me

A long time dream, soon to come true

Hi there, Iâ€™m Alex! I love to share kindness by building useful things with code.

My first introduction to programming was when I was 13, during some school holidays. I was completely absorbed in a tutorial on the legendary SiteDuZero website (this one). I was reading on an old Sony Ericsson phone, with 2G internet access, without an actual computer at hand, or any electricity during the day.

More than a decade later, I found myself constantly steering towards technical stuff. During my first internship in Spain, creating a macro in Excel seemed on the level of magic in the eyes of my peers! And at one point, it all clicked. Right after finishing my curriculum at EMLYON Business School, I decided to learn Python and SQL, which was pivotal in helping me land my first full-time job in an analytics role.

As I was working with a variety of other tools as part of my work, I realized I had the skills to leverage my coding skills to bring more to the table. So I threw together a Python script that would automatically update some reportings, that I eventually scheduled using the 2,000 free monthly usage minutes on Github Actions. Who knew a CI/CD tool would be useful that early in my journey!

As I grew increasingly eager to solve business problems with code, I decided to learn web development. Being self-taught in JavaScript now gives me an additional edge to be creative in problem-solving and do cool things at work. Now, I intend to make development my future career, and work in a company where I can grow alongside a team who will raise my standards for what is expected of a web application, and help them achieve company goals.
